About the project

Our Aim Assisted Robot is able to detect the red color and adjust its servo positioning accordingly so that the laser is pointing straight at the red target. Additionally, when the red target is detected and found, the LED is turned on. This contributes to the area of target detection and taking action based on that target. It can be used as an insect or vermin trap, home protection, or any other process that requires the detection of a target and its position.
